About Me

With a BS Degree in Biochemistry from the UW, I am currently a sales engineer for lab scale tangential flow membrane research equipment, and I consult with customers to explore best fits for their project needs.

I've developed an interest in web design/development to create webpages and web apps which are currently lacking in my industry, so that better resources can be used to help others that follow similar lines of work.

If you would like to learn more about me, please visit the Contact page at the top right of the screen.
